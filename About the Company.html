About Us.



Sakura is a business that creates high-quality and highly sustainable products, making it stylish and environmentally friendly. As a sustainable fashion brand, we want to influence the youth to make wiser decisions about our future. Environmental problems have plagued our planet and one of the most contributing factors to it is the fashion industry. As a brand, we see the importance of caring for our planet through the use of fashion. Our bags are made of biodegradable products without the use of harsh chemicals that may affect the planet. We offer products for men, women, and gender-neutral individuals. We wish for you to stay fashionable and to go sustainable.
